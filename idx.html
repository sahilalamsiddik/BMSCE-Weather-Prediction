Weather Wonders

⚡ Lightning Speed

A single bolt of lightning contains enough energy to toast 160,000 slices of bread. That is one powerful breakfast!

❄️ Unique Snowflakes

While often called unique, scientists have actually found "identical twin" snowflakes in rare atmospheric conditions. Still, most are one-of-a-kind!

🌪️ Fastest Winds

The highest wind speed ever recorded was 253 mph during Tropical Cyclone Olivia in 1996. That's faster than a Formula 1 car!

🌡️ Nature's Thermometer

You can tell the temperature by counting cricket chirps! Count how many times a cricket chirps in 15 seconds and add 37 to get the Temp in Fahrenheit.

🌈 Double Rainbows

A double rainbow occurs when light is reflected twice inside a raindrop. The colors of the second arc are always inverted!

🏜️ Rainless Deserts

In the Atacama Desert in Chile, some places haven't seen a drop of rain in over 400 years. Talk about a dry spell!
